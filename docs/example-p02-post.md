## Reddit p-02 — Feature-Fokus: Net Worth Tracker

**Subreddit:** r/Notion  
**Post-ID:** notion-personal-finance-r-02

**Titel:**
```
My Notion net worth tracker — one formula, one table, monthly snapshots
```

**Body:**
```
Started logging my net worth monthly in Notion about six months ago.
It's the one habit that's changed how I think about money.

The setup:
- One row per month
- Assets column (cash + investments + property value)
- Liabilities column (loans + credit card balance)
- Net worth = Assets − Liabilities (formula, updates automatically)
- % change from last month — so I can see if I'm moving in the right direction

The trick: I do it on the 1st of every month, takes 5 minutes.
After three months you start to see the line go up (or know why it isn't).

This is part of a bigger Notion finance template I built — budget vs. actual,
savings goals with progress bars, transaction log. All in one workspace,
no bank connection, no third-party servers.

Happy to share the net worth formula if anyone wants it.

→ https://tinycraft.gumroad.com/l/qwpidn
```


**Farbpalette:**
- Hintergrund: `linear-gradient(135deg, #0f0f0f 0%, #1a1a2e 100%)`
- Haupttext: `#ffffff`
- Akzent: `#6366f1` (Indigo — ein Begriff pro Headline)
- Nebentext: `rgba(255,255,255,0.4)` und `rgba(255,255,255,0.6)`

**Typografie:**
- Font: Inter oder Helvetica Neue
- Headline: 88px, font-weight 800, line-height 1.0, letter-spacing -3px
- Brand: 14px, letter-spacing 4px, uppercase
- CTA: 18px, letter-spacing 1px

**Schlüsselregel für Headline:**
- Maximal 4–6 Wörter
- Zwei Zeilen, zweite Zeile endet mit dem Indigo-Begriff
- Kein Fragezeichen, keine Ausrufezeichen — nüchterne Aussage
- Beispiele:
  - "Build the habit. *Keep the streak.*"
  - "Know your money. *Keep your data.*"
  - "Every idea. *One place.*"
  - "Plan your week. *Own your time.*"
